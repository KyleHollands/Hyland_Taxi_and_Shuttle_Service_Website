About the Company

Hyland Taxi, originally serving Haliburton County and the surrounding area, is excited to announce our new transportation service from Haliburton to Toronto! This route, previously managed by Tok Coachlines and Entertainment Tours, faced challenges due to decreased and inconsistent ridership. To ensure we can keep this service running smoothly for the long term, we're using a smaller, 22-seater bus. While fares have increased slightly, this change helps us maintain a sustainable, flexible, and reliable travel option for you. We're committed to providing you with the best service possible!
