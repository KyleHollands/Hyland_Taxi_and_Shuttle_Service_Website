Toronto to Haliburton Transportation

A new transportation service is available between Toronto and Haliburton.

  • On Wednesdays and Fridays, the shuttle departs from Yorkdale Mall at 12:00 PM and arrives at the Shamrock Center in Haliburton at 3:30 PM, with the return trip to Toronto departing at 4:30 PM.
  • On Sundays and holidays, the shuttle departs from Yorkdale Mall at 1:00 PM and arrives at the Shamrock Center in Haliburton at 4:30 PM, with the return trip to Toronto departing at 5:30 PM.
Important Information
  • Reserve your seat in advance by calling dispatch at the bottom of the page. Provide your boarding location, destination, and number of passengers. Failure to comply may result in being denied boarding.
  • Currently, only cash is accepted, payable upon boarding. Call dispatch for fare costs.
  • Limit your belongings to one small carry-on and one piece of luggage per person for everyone's comfort.
